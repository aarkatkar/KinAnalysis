BMB471 - Kinetic Analysis

Hello future biochemist! You may use this site to record the slope of enzyme assays performed in BMB471. Remember that for each assay, you must record the "Start(s)", "End (s)", and "ΔA/min" values. The curvature (% variability in slope) is just presented to verify that you have a sufficiently linear region. Good luck!
